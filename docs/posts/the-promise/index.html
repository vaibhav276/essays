    The promise

    Posted at — Aug 21, 2020

    I sit down for a moment just to try and unwind I promise myself that I’ll make it work

    It might not be obvious I might not see where I need not understand but the promise is always there

    The food is a promise to fill my belly The joke is a promise to make a chuckle

    What good is a laugh if it doesn’t bring joy I keep telling myself “Don’t fail to try”

    I am but a child often late to remind that promises are made and broken all the time
